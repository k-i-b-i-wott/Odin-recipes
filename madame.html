Preparation of Croque Madame

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 tablespoons salted butter, softened and divided
  • 1 1/2 tables spoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 ounces shredded Gruyere cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper, or taste
  • 1/4teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 4 slices white bread, lightly toasted
  • 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
  • 2 thin slices Black Forest ham
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh dill, or to taste

PROCEDURE

  1. Set an oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and preheat at the oven's boiler
  2. Melt 1 1/2 tablespoons of butter in a saucepan over medium-low heat.Whisk in flour
    and stir until slightly brown, about 3 minutes . Whisk in milk ;bring to a boil, stirring constantly.
    Once boiling, reduce heat immediately and simmer for 5 minutes.
    Add 1/2 of the Gruyere cheese, salt, pepper and nutmeg; heat until cheese is melted, about 2 minutes.
    Remove from the heat and set asied.
  3. Spread about 1 teaspoon butter on 2 pieces of bread. Spread 1 tablespoon cheese sauce
    on top and layer each with a ham.spread 1 teaspoon butter on the remaining 2 pieces of bread,
    the spread Dijon mustard ob top and sprinkle with remaining Gruyere. Transfer all 4 pieces to a baking sheet.
  4. Broil the preheated oven until cheese is almost melted. Remove from the oven, place toast
    slices together to make 2 sandwiches and pour remaining cheese sauce on top of each sandwhich.Return to the broiler until sauce to brown
  5. While the sandwiches are boiling, heat remaining butter in a nonstick skillet over medium-high heat.
    Crack 2 eggs into the skillet, keeping them completely separate from one another.
    Season with salt and pepper. Add water, cover and baste unitl whites are set but yolks are still runny
  6. Top each sandwhich with an egg and sprinkle with dill, if using. serve immediately
