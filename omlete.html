Preparation of Bacon Omlete Cups

INGREDIENTS

  • cooking spray
  • 3 cups frozen shredded hash brown potatoes, thawed
  • 3 tablesspoons butter, melted
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoonfreshley groung black pepper
  • 2 cups shredded Mexican-style four-cheese blend
  • 6 large eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1/4 cup chopped red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup chooped red bell pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper
  • 6 slicebacon, crisp-cooked amd chopped
  • chooped fresh chives, for garnish

PROCEDURE

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees. Grease 12 muffin cups with cooking spray
  2. Wrap hash browns in a clean kitchen towel and squeeze to remove as much moisture as possible.
    Stir together hash browns, melted butter, salt, and black pepper in large bowl.
    Press about 1/4 cup of the hash brown mixture into bottom and up the sides of each prepared muffin cup
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until lightly browned,18 to 20 minutes
  4. Meanwhile, stir together cheese,eggs, bell pepper, and crushed red pepper.
  5. Remove muffin from the oven. Reduce oven temperature to 400 degrees F.
    Top hash browns with half of the bacon. Top with the egg mixture and the remaining bacon.
    B ake untill a knife inserted into centers comes out clean, 13 to 15 minutes. Garnish with chives
