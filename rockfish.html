Preparation of Baked Asian Rockfish

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 rockfish filets
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon finely minced fresh ginger
  • 1 table spoon honey
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tabespoon lime juice
  • 1/4 cup diagonally thinly sliced scallions

PROCEDURE

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F.Line a shallow baking pan with aluminium foil, and spray cooking spray.
    Place filets onto the prepared baking pan
  2. In small bowl combine sesame oil, soy sauce, lime juice, honey, ginger, garlic and pepperflakes. Spoon mixture evenly over top of filets
  3. Bake in the preheated oven untill fish flakes easily with a fork ,10 to 15 minutes.
    Sprinkle filets with scallions bafore serving.
